Bookshelf Chats

The 2020 GISConnect video series features senior-level career mentors, highlighting outstanding women in the field of GIScience. Researchers share favorite books from their bookshelf or a journal article that inspired them. Community members can use this valuable teaching and mentoring media series in class and share videos with friends.
